Hi, I'm Esa

I'm a digital marketer who loves experimenting with new ideas and sharing what I learn along the way. This site is my public workspace where I document projects, experiments, and insights.

What I Do

My work spans several areas of digital marketing and web development:

  • Digital Marketing - Strategy, campaigns, and growth experiments
  • SEO & Content - Including programmatic SEO experiments
  • Side Projects - Building tools and testing business ideas

Why "Building in Public"?

I believe the best way to learn is by doing—and sharing that process openly. By documenting my experiments (including the failures), I hope to:

  • Force myself to think more clearly about what I'm doing
  • Help others who might be exploring similar ideas
  • Connect with like-minded people
  • Create a record of my learning journey

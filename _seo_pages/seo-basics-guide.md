Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is essential for anyone who wants their content to be discovered online. This guide covers the fundamentals you need to know.

## What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It's the practice of optimizing your website and content to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs) for relevant queries.

When someone searches for something on Google, SEO determines which pages appear first. Higher rankings mean more visibility and traffic.

## How Search Engines Work

Understanding how search engines work helps you optimize effectively:

### 1. Crawling

Search engines use automated programs called "crawlers" or "spiders" to discover web pages. They follow links from page to page, discovering new content.

### 2. Indexing

Once discovered, pages are analyzed and stored in a massive database (the index). The search engine tries to understand what each page is about.

### 3. Ranking

When someone searches, the algorithm determines which indexed pages best answer the query, then ranks them accordingly.

## On-Page SEO Fundamentals

On-page SEO refers to optimizations you make directly on your website:

### Title Tags

The title tag is crucial for both rankings and click-through rates:
- Include your target keyword
- Keep it under 60 characters
- Make it compelling

### Meta Descriptions

While not a direct ranking factor, meta descriptions affect click-through rates:
- Summarize the page content
- Include a call to action
- Keep it under 160 characters

### Headers (H1, H2, H3)

Use headers to structure your content:
- One H1 per page (usually the title)
- Use H2s for main sections
- Use H3s for subsections

### Content Quality

The most important on-page factor:
- Answer the user's query thoroughly
- Provide unique value
- Write for humans first, search engines second

### Internal Linking

Link to other relevant pages on your site:
- Helps search engines discover content
- Passes authority between pages
- Improves user experience

## Technical SEO Basics

Technical SEO ensures search engines can crawl and index your site:

- **Page Speed**: Fast-loading pages rank better
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Essential for modern SEO
- **HTTPS**: Secure sites are preferred
- **XML Sitemap**: Helps search engines find your pages

## Getting Started with SEO

SEO is a long-term investment. Here's how to begin:

1. **Research keywords** people search for in your niche
2. **Create quality content** that answers those queries
3. **Optimize on-page elements** (titles, headers, etc.)
4. **Build internal links** between related content
5. **Monitor results** and iterate

The best way to learn SEO is through practice. Start with one piece of content, optimize it, and observe the results.
